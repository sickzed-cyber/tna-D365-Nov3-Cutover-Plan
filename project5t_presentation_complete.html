Risk Reduction Measures

September 15 - October 24, 2025

Risk Reduction Timeline

  • Vendor Delivery Management: Last deliveries accepted on October 24, 2025. Normal operations resume on November 4, 2025
  • Customer Order Fulfillment: Early shipment of customer orders to avoid the blackout period. Prioritize orders with delivery dates during cutover window
  • Design Release Freeze: Freeze on new product designs from October 24, 2025. Designs held in Vault for release into D365 from November 4, 2025
  • Manual Transaction Processing: Templates for urgent transactions during blackout period. Manual entries to be loaded after November 4, 2025

Business Impact Assessment

  • Reduced transaction backlog
  • Minimized customer impact
  • Decreased data entry errors
  • Shorter stabilization period

Cutover Execution

October 29 - November 3, 2025

Cutover Timeline

  • System Freeze - October 28, 2025: AX close and final data extraction
  • Data Migration - October 29-31, 2025: Transfer and validation of all data to D365
  • Configuration & Testing - November 1-2, 2025: Final system configuration and integration testing
  • Go-Live - November 3, 2025: Synchronized global implementation across all entities

Command Center Structure

  • 24/7 Support Team
  • Technical Specialists
  • Executive Oversight
  • Real-time Monitoring

Post-Go-Live Activities (November 4+)

  • New design releases from Vault to D365
  • Vendor deliveries resume normal operations
  • Hypercare support and system stabilization
