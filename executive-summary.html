Executive Summary

Project 5T: Transforming Global Operations

tna team transform transition together

Project Overview & Strategic Intent

Project 5T represents a transformational initiative to unify TNA Solutions' global operations through the implementation of Microsoft Dynamics 365, creating a single, integrated platform that will drive unprecedented efficiency, collaboration, and growth across our worldwide network of companies.

Strategic Vision

Transform from disconnected companies with disparate legacy systems to a fully connected, intercompany global operation powered by integrated D365 DNA, enabling seamless collaboration, real-time visibility, and accelerated decision-making across all business units.

Project Scope & Scale

  • Global Implementation: Unified D365 platform across all TNA group companies
  • Multi-Regional Deployment: Coordinated rollout across manufacturing sites and trading entities
  • Comprehensive Integration: CRM, Financial Operations, Supply Chain, and Manufacturing modules
  • Advanced Warehouse Management: Migration from Advanced to Basic Warehouse processes
  • Third-Party Integrations: Citi Bank, Vault Design System, and partner platforms
Oct 2 Go-Live Date 2025

Modern, scalable cloud infrastructure ensures 99.9% uptime, automatic updates, and global accessibility while reducing IT infrastructure costs and complexity.

Native integration with Microsoft ecosystem (Office 365, Teams, Power Platform) and extensive third-party connectivity capabilities streamline workflows and enhance productivity.

Advanced Analytics

Built-in Power BI integration provides real-time dashboards, predictive analytics, and AI-driven insights for informed decision-making across all business functions.

Mobile-First Experience

Responsive design and dedicated mobile applications enable workforce productivity from anywhere, supporting remote work and field operations effectively.

Enterprise Security

Microsoft's comprehensive security framework includes advanced threat protection, data encryption, compliance management, and identity governance capabilities.

AI & Automation

Embedded artificial intelligence and process automation capabilities reduce manual tasks, improve accuracy, and enable intelligent business process optimization.
